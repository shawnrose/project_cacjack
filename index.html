Welcome to Cactus Jack Music

Cactus Jack and His Band of Outlaws are quickly becoming one of the most entertainf band to see in Fort Worth. With their rowdy style and down home roots, they know how to have a good time and play some damn fine music. Chris Wood, frontman for Cactus Jack has been playing around the Fort Worth music scene for many years. With his revolving band line up, he has assembled some of Fort Worths' finest players to grace the stage with him. Sit back and pop a top, Cactus Jack's about to get rowdy!

Cactus Jack and His Band of Outlaws:

  • Chris Wood - Guitar and Vocals
  • Shawn Rose - Banjo
  • Greg New - Bass
  • Shelly Duell - Fiddle
